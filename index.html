About Me

I'm a London Based iOS and tvOS developer currently working with the YOOX NET-A-PORTER Group where I focus on building features for the Net-A-Porter iOS app as well as the MR PORTER tvOS app.

Having worked on the Net-A-Porter on a daily basis for the past two years, I am used to working on apps with a large user base and have been exposed to a number of industry standard tools including all major iOS frameworks, analytics services including Adobe and Fabric, Version control tools as well as releasing and monitoring through iTunes connect.

As well as the Net-A-Porter iOS app, I also worked within a small team to produce the worlds first shoppable tvOS app, the Mr Porter Apple TV allows user to browse through the full library of videos on Mr Porter's editorial and also allows user's to buy any of the items they see by connecting their phones.

Throughout my time as a developer, I have developed using Objective-C, but since the release of Swift 2, I have migrated my skills in order to make Swift my primary programming language. I'm also proficient in using Node.js as well as front-end languages such as HTML, CSS and vanilla Javascript which I have used for hacks, projects and websites.
I am constantly looking to upskill and test new tools and languages, most of my projects can be found on my Github Page available here. Some of the capabailities acquired through my three years of experience include:

  • - Monitoring performance through anlytics services such as Omniture, Google Analytics and Fabric
  • - Deploying iOS and tvOS apps throught the App Store
  • - Developing applications using Node.js
  • - Deploying and monitoring websites using Heroku
